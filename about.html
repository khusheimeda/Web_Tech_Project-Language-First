ABOUT

This website is to help you learn and improve your language skills. It's specially focused on improving your writing, grammar and diction.

How it works
1. Every user that signs up with us must be proficient in writing skills at least one language, and wish to learn at least one other language.
2. After you sign up, you can share your queries in assorted domains that you'll find on the left side of your screen.
3. Other users who claim to be proficient in that language will answer your queries and help you out!
4. You can do the same for others and help the community grow :)
5. You get points for every query asked and answered and you can check out your cumulative score in your profile.

Have fun learning and remember, Language First!

NOTE: It's currently available only for German, French and Spanish.
